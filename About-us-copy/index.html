About Us

Founded in 1996, Fresh Supplies is a leading supplier to the Hospitality, Catering, and Bakery trade channels in the UAE and Oman.

Fresh Supplies LLC is a UAE-based company, representing leading International Dairy, Bakery Ingredients, and Confectionery brands. All of which proudly allow us to deliver a sales revenue stream in excess of $US 25 million per annum.

Our product range is highly synergistic and focused on delivering quality and efficient solutions to our 385+ customer base who represent the elite of their respective trade channels.

We are a HACCP Certified organization, where Innovation, Service, and Quality are our key mantras.

Our Operation

We offer a 6 day per week delivery service, with a maximum delivery time of 24hrs from receipt of order.

This is supported by a fleet of 12 climate controlled vehicles, with a dedicated, well trained supply chain team who manage 20,000 sq ft of climate controlled warehousing.

Our sales team members are technically capable and competent, and committed to ensuring client satisfaction across the UAE and Oman.
