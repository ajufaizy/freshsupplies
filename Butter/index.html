Crafted from high-quality cream sourced from milk, this Unsalted Bulk Butter is a testament to excellence in dairy production. Certified by Westland Milk Products, New Zealand, Westgold butter adheres to Halal and Kosher standards, ensuring that it meets the dietary requirements of diverse culinary traditions.
