Moreover, scratch-made crème pâtissier has a limited shelf life, necessitating daily preparation to ensure freshness. This is where Delice’s instant custard powder steps in to revolutionize the culinary process.

Delice instant custard powder offers a cold process solution that simplifies and expedites the custard-making process. The convenience of Delice instant custard powder allows chefs to produce custard as needed, minimizing wastage, and reducing both time and cost expenditure. Its quick preparation time saves valuable minutes in a busy kitchen, enabling chefs to focus on other intricate aspects of their culinary creations.

One of the standout features of Delice instant custard powder is its ability to deliver a natural flavor profile, ensuring that the custard base maintains the authentic taste of scratch-made crème pâtissier. Whether it's for fillings, pastry creams, or dessert bases, Delice instant custard powder proves to be a versatile and efficient solution.
