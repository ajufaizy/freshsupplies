CREA Chocolate

CREA Chocolate is a renowned brand that offers a delightful range of chocolate products with a distinct flavor profiles. Originating from Italy, CREA Chocolate has garnered a reputation for its sweeter taste, appealing to chocolate lovers around the world. What sets CREA Chocolate apart is its meticulous control over the entire chocolate-making process, from bean to bar. With four decades of experience, this family-managed company ensures quality and consistency in every step of production.

 

The diverse range of CREA Chocolate products caters to various preferences and culinary applications. Dark chocolate enthusiasts can indulge in the rich flavors of the 60% and 73% cocoa variants, while those who prefer a milder taste can savor the Milk chocolate options, available in 33% and 36% cocoa content. For a creamy and delicate option, CREA offers White chocolate with a 30% cocoa base. The chocolates come in convenient coin forms, facilitating easy measuring /melting in recipes.
